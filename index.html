Welcome to Vibes
A private space to track where your friendships and connections actually stand. Only you see your ratings — nothing is shared.
📊
How the Vibe Score works
Rate people where you want the relationship to go, not just where it is right now. Your rating is always private. You only see a Vibe Score with someone once you've rated them too — then it shows up as the lower of the two ratings. Your higher rating stays yours. Use it as an intention, not a verdict.

When things get serious
Once your Vibe Score with someone hits Dating or higher, your romantic scale gets locked for everyone else — and at the same time, anyone viewing your profile sees that you're in a relationship. The app recognises you're in something and takes you off the table. No action needed — it happens automatically.
💬
A use case
Sam and Alex secretly like each other. Sam, ready to date, marks their vibe at Dating. Alex, wanting to take things a bit slower, marks Talking. They would have never known they liked each other — but now they're talking.
🙌
No pressure, no labels
Vibes isn't here to define anyone or create pressure around where things stand. Scores are private — no one else sees them. Don't let the numbers box anyone in. The app works for you, not the other way around.
